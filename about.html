ABOUT

A former art student who pursued various disciplines including fashion design, graphic design, fine arts and illustration, I have always been passionate about creating meaningful experiences.

I am currently a undergraduate research assistant working on ergonomic research, user friendly product development, and UXUI design at The University of Michigan where I am pursuing a degree in computer science. I am exploring a blend of these two passions through my experience in the website team of the Korean International Student Association. Here, I contributed to developing a fully responsive, production level website by implementing CRUD applications with various industry skills including an AWS backend and Next.js frontend. Currently my team is working on converting the website to React Native to deploy a mobile application version. These opportunities have allowed me to explore my different yet synonymous passions in art and technology.
